cat in my lap
paws across my wrist
heavy
she doesn't seem to mind the dance that is me typing

sunlight streams
across the floor
up the walls
makes the plies of books
look sculptural

birds singing
outside my house
quietly
adding their music
to the day

leaves larger
more green
puffs of tender membrane
umbrella out
stretch to the sun
