I was asked to provide the list of food sources that I mentioned in my FutureRuby presentation. So here they are. My source for the information is Prescription for Nutritional Healing by Balch & Balch. I used the third edition but I believe the fourth edition is the most current. This is one of my most used reference books when it comes to nutrition.

Calcium
:: dairy foods,salmon (with bones), sardines, seafood, dark green leafy vegetables, almonds, asparagus, blackstrap molasses, brewer's yeast, broccoli, buttermilk, cabbage, carob, cheese, collards, dandelion greens, dulse, figs, filberts, goat's milk, kale, kelp, milk, mustard greens, oats, prunes, sesame seeds, soybeans, tofu, turnip greens, watercress, whey and yogurt

Iron
:: eggs, fish, liver, meat, poultry, green leafy vegetables, whole grains, enriched breads & cereals, almonds, avocados, beets, blackstrap molasses, brewer's yeast, dates, dulse, kelp, kidney beans, lima beans, lentils, millet, peaches, pears, prunes, pumpkin, raisins, rice bran, wheat bran, sesame seeds, soybeans, watercress

Sodium
:: most foods

Potassium
:: dairy foods, fish, fruit, legumes, meat, poultry, vegetables, whole grains, apricots, avocados, bananas, lima beans, blackstrap molasses, brewer's yeast, brown rice, dates, dulse, figs, dried fruit, garlic, nuts, potatoes, raisins, spinach, winter squash, tortula yeast, wheat bran, yams, yogurt

B Vitamins
:: brown rice, egg yolks, fish, legumes, liver, peanuts, peas, pork, poultry, rice bran, wheat germ, whole grains, asparagus, avocados, brewer's yeast, broccoli, brussel sprouts, cheese, currants, dandelion greens, dates, dulse, kelp, molasses, milk, nuts, oatmeal, plums, prunes, raisins, royal jelly, spinach, spirulina, sunflower seeds, walnuts, watercress

Vitamin D
:: fish liver oil, fatty saltwater fish, dairy products, eggs, butter, dandelion greens, halibut, liver, milk, oatmeal, salmon, sardines, sweet potatoes, tuna, vegetable oil; your body can also manufacture Vitamin D from sunshine on your skin

Essential Fatty Acids
:: emu oil, fish oil, flaxseeds & flaxseed oil, hempseeds & hempseed oil, grapeseed oil, borage oil, olive oil
